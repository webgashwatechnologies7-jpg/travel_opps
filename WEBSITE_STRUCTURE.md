# Centura Travel CRM - Website Structure & Roadmap
**Goal:** A high-conversion marketing website to sell the CRM to travel agencies.

## üìä Summary
*   **Total Pages:** ~8-10 Pages (Recommended for SEO & Premium feel)
*   **Total Forms:** 3 Main Forms (Lead Capture & Conversion)

---

## 1. üìë Pages Breakdown (Detailed)

### A. Core Pages (The Face of the Brand)
1.  **Home Page (Landing Page)**
    *   *Purpose:* The "Hook". Explains what it is, why it's better, and gets them to click "Start Trial".
    *   *Sections:* Hero (Headline + Image), Problem/Solution, Top features preview, Testimonials, Pricing Snippet, Final CTA.
2.  **About Us**
    *   *Purpose:* Build Trust. Agencies buy people, not just software.
    *   *Sections:* Company mission, "Why we built this", Certifications (if any).
3.  **Contact Support**
    *   *Purpose:* Reassurance that help is available.
    *   *Sections:* Office Address, Email/Phone, Map, "Send a Message" form.

### B. Feature Pages (Dedicated "Sales" Pages)
*Why separate pages? Better Google Ranking (SEO) and focused selling.*

4.  **Feature 1: Smart CRM & Lead Management**
    *   *Focus:* Organizing leads, assigning staff, automated follow-ups.
    *   *Key Visual:* A screenshot of the Kanban board or Lead List.
5.  **Feature 2: Itinerary Builder (The "Hero" Feature)**
    *   *Focus:* Speed. "Create a 5-day trip in 2 minutes". Offline capability.
    *   *Key Visual:* Video/GIF of dragging and dropping a hotel into a day plan.
6.  **Feature 3: Operations & Automations**
    *   *Focus:* Vouchers, Invoices, Auto-emailing clients.
    *   *Key Visual:* An example of a generated professional Voucher/Invoice.
7.  **Feature 4: Financial Suite**
    *   *Focus:* GST Reports, Profit & Loss, Expense tracking.
    *   *Key Visual:* A clean graph showing "Monthly Profit".

### C. Conversion Pages
8.  **Pricing Plans**
    *   *Purpose:* Clear comparison of Logic (Startup vs Growth vs Enterprise).
    *   *Sections:* Toggle (Monthly/Yearly), Comparison Table, FAQ.
9.  **Book a Demo (Calendar)**
    *   *Purpose:* For high-ticket clients who want to talk to a human.
    *   *Sections:* Embed a calendar (Calendly) or a detailed form.

### D. Legal (Required for Payment Gateways)
10. **Privacy Policy / Terms of Service**
    *   *Purpose:* Mandatory for trust and payment gateway approval.

## 2. üìù Forms Breakdown

### Form 1: "Get Started" / Sign Up (The Money Form)
*   **Location:** Pricing Page, Top Navigation Buttons.
*   **Fields:**
    *   Full Name
    *   Business Email (Validation required)
    *   Phone Number (OTP Verification if needed)
    *   Company Name
    *   Password Setup

### Form 2: "Request a Demo" (High-Value Leads)
*   **Location:** Header, Footer, "Enterprise" Plan section.
*   **Fields:**
    *   Name & Email
    *   Team Size (Dropdown: 1-5, 5-20, 20+)
    *   Primary Requirement (Dropdown: CRM, Itinerary, Accounting)
    *   "Best time to call"

### Form 3: General "Contact Us"
*   **Location:** Contact Page.
*   **Fields:** Name, Email, Subject, Message.
